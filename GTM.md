# PlumbLead.ai — Go-to-Market

## Pricing

| | Setup | Monthly | Annual Value |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Standard** | $2,500 | $500/mo | $8,500 first year |
| **Premium** (add chatbot + advanced analytics) | $3,500 | $750/mo | $12,500 first year |

**ROI Framing:**
- Average plumbing service call: $350-500
- Average water heater install: $2,000-3,500
- If PlumbLead books just 2 extra jobs/month → $700-7,000 in revenue
- System pays for itself with ONE extra water heater install

## Phase 1: Warm Outreach (Week 1-2)

### Ryan's Local Network

Target: Plumbing company owners Ryan knows personally in Arizona.

**Text Template:**
```
Hey [name] — quick question. How fast does your team
respond when a lead comes in from your website or Google?

I built something that responds in under 60 seconds with
a price range and books the appointment automatically.
Already working for HVAC guys. Building the plumbing
version now and need 2-3 plumbers to test it free for
30 days. Interested?
```

**Key moves:**
- Don't sell. Offer a free pilot.
- First 3 clients = free 30-day trial (they become case studies + testimonials)
- After 30 days, show them the data: "You booked X jobs through PlumbLead. Want to keep it running?"

### The Demo That Sells Itself

"Go to your website right now and fill out your own contact form. Time how long it takes for someone on your team to respond."

Then: "Now try this." → They fill out the PlumbLead demo → get a text in 30 seconds with a quote range and booking link.

The demo IS the pitch. Nothing else needed.

## Phase 2: Local Outreach (Week 3-4)

### Target List: Phoenix Metro Plumbing Companies

**Ideal customer:**
- 2-20 trucks
- Running Google LSA or PPC ads (spending money on leads already)
- Has a website with a contact form
- Using ServiceTitan or Housecall Pro
- Owner is accessible (not a franchise/corporate)

**How to find them:**
- Google "plumber Phoenix" — first 20 results in LSA/ads = spending money
- Yelp top-rated plumbers in Phoenix, Scottsdale, Mesa, Chandler, Glendale, Peoria
- ServiceTitan customer directory
- Local plumbing trade groups / Arizona Registrar of Contractors

**Cold outreach (email):**
```
Subject: You lost a lead at 2am last night

[First name] —

Last night someone in [city] had a pipe burst. They Googled
"emergency plumber near me" and found your site.

They filled out your contact form at 2:14 AM.

Your team responded at 8:47 AM. By then they'd already
booked someone else.

PlumbLead responds in under 60 seconds — 24/7 — with a
price range and books the appointment. No humans needed.

Want to see it work on your own website? Takes 5 minutes.

— Ryan
PlumbLead.ai
```

**Cold outreach (walk-in):**
- Arizona advantage: you can literally walk into plumbing company offices
- Bring your phone, demo the system live
- "Fill out this form and watch what happens on your phone"

## Phase 3: Scale (Month 2+)

- Case studies from Phase 1 clients → content for website
- Google Ads targeting "plumbing lead management software"
- Partnerships with plumbing supply houses (they see every plumber)
- ServiceTitan / Housecall Pro marketplace listing
- YouTube: "How many leads is your plumbing company losing?" (screen recorded demo)

## Sales Objections

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "I already have a CRM" | "We plug into it. ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro — PlumbLead sends qualified, pre-quoted leads directly into your existing system" |
| "AI can't quote plumbing jobs" | "You're right — we don't give exact quotes. We give ranges based on YOUR prices. It sets expectations and books the call. Your plumber confirms the exact price on-site." |
| "I don't trust AI talking to my customers" | "Every message uses your company name, your tone. We set it up together. And you see every conversation in real-time." |
| "Too expensive" | "What's one water heater install worth to you? $2,000-3,500? The system costs $500/month. It pays for itself with one job." |
| "$2,500 setup is steep" | "That covers building your custom quote tool, loading your price book, integrating your CRM, and training the AI on your business. It's a done-for-you buildout." |
| "I'll think about it" | "Totally fair. In the meantime — fill out your own contact form tonight at midnight and see how long it takes your team to respond. Then we'll talk." |

## Metrics to Track

- Outreach sent vs. demos booked (target: 20% demo rate)
- Demos vs. trials started (target: 50%)
- Trials vs. paid conversion (target: 70%)
- Monthly recurring revenue
- Client churn rate
- Average jobs booked per client per month via PlumbLead
