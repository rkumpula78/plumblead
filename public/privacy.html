Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

PlumbLead.ai ("we," "us," or "our") operates the plumblead.ai website and related SMS messaging services. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information: Name, phone number, email address, and zip code submitted through our quote request forms.
  • Service details: Type of plumbing service requested, property information, and any details you provide about your project.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, time on site, and browser type (collected automatically via cookies).

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide instant plumbing service quotes based on your request.
  • To send SMS messages confirming your quote and connecting you with a local plumber.
  • To generate water quality reports based on your zip code using publicly available municipal water data.
  • To improve our services and user experience.

SMS Messaging

By submitting a quote request on our website, you consent to receive SMS messages related to your service request. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

To opt out: Reply STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance.

We will not send marketing messages unless you separately opt in.

Information Sharing

We share your contact and service request information only with the plumbing contractor you are matched with to fulfill your service request. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS) and secure data storage.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing ryan@plumblead.ai.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
