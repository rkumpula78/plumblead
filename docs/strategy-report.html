🚫 What NOT to Do

  • Don't build a septic marketplace first. Two-sided marketplace cold start is brutal. Expand there naturally from your plumber network.
  • Don't try to be a CRM. ServiceTitan and Housecall Pro own that layer. PlumbLead sits IN FRONT of them.
  • Don't go national before proving local. Phoenix first. Seattle second. Template and scale after.
  • Don't build Voice AI yet. High effort, high complexity. SMS gets 80% of the value.
  • Don't spread across trades. Stay plumbing. Water intelligence moat only works in plumbing.
  • Don't sell irrigation. License issues, trade boundaries, low margin. Referral only.
