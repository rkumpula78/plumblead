Program Description

PlumbLead.ai provides an instant quote and lead management platform for plumbing contractors. Homeowners submit service requests through our website and receive automated SMS messages with quote estimates and appointment scheduling.

SMS Terms

By submitting a quote request form on plumblead.ai, you agree to receive automated SMS messages related to your plumbing service request. This includes:

  • Quote confirmation messages
  • Appointment scheduling messages
  • Follow-up messages related to your service request

Message frequency: Typically 1-5 messages per service request.

Message and data rates may apply. Check with your carrier for details.

Opt-Out

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message. You will not receive further messages unless you submit a new request.
