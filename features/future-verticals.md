## Pest & Termite Control
- **Why it works:** High search volume, recurring revenue (quarterly treatments), seasonal urgency, small operators with bad online presence
- **AZ angle:** Scorpions, termites (especially subterranean in Phoenix), bark scorpions are the #1 searched pest in the Valley
- **Model:** Same speed-to-lead + instant quote engine, different service categories
- **What transfers:** Quote tool, SMS workflow, CRM integration, dashboard
- **What doesn't:** Water intelligence (no equivalent moat — would need pest-specific intelligence like termite risk by neighborhood/soil type)
- **When:** Month 4-6 after plumbing model is proven

## Commercial/Industrial Service Lead Tool
- **Concept:** Speed-to-lead for commercial service companies — HVAC maintenance contracts, boiler service, compressed air, water treatment (industrial), electrical PM
- **Why interesting:** Higher contract values ($5K-50K+/yr), longer sales cycles but stickier once won, less competition for commercial lead gen (everyone targets residential)
- **Ryan's edge:** Brenntag KAE experience = he understands B2B sales cycles, procurement, and industrial service relationships better than any SaaS founder in this space
- **What's different from residential:** RFQ process, maintenance agreements, compliance/regulatory drivers, multi-site contracts
- **Potential:** Very real, but different GTM (LinkedIn outreach, trade shows, industry-specific) vs residential (Google/Facebook ads)
- **When:** Evaluate after residential PlumbLead proves the core engine works. Could be a separate brand entirely.
